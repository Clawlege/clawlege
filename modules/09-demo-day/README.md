# Module 8: Demo Day

**⏱ Time: ~30 minutes | 🔴 Difficulty: Advanced | 💻 Coding: None**

You built something. Now show the world.

---

## What You'll Learn

- **Storytelling** — How to present a technical project to any audience
- **Demo structure** — The problem→solution→demo→impact framework
- **Live demos** — Tips for demos that don't crash (and what to do when they do)
- **Community** — Finding your people in the AI agent community
- **What's next** — Where to go from here

## Module Outline

### Lesson 1: Tell Your Story
- Why you built this agent (the problem)
- What it does (the solution)
- The "before and after" — life without vs. with your agent
- Keep it human: people care about stories, not specs

### Lesson 2: The Perfect Demo
- The 3-minute demo structure
- Setting up a reliable demo environment
- What to show and what to skip
- Backup plans for when things go wrong
- Recording your demo (screen capture + narration)

### Lesson 3: Share Your Work
- Where to post: GitHub, ClawHub, social media, forums
- Writing an announcement that gets attention
- Creating a 30-second video or GIF preview
- Engaging with feedback (positive and negative)

### Lesson 4: What's Next
- Advanced OpenClaw features to explore
- The AI agent ecosystem — what else is out there
- Contributing to Clawlege (yes, you can!)
- Building agents for others — freelance, open source, startup ideas
- The future of AI agents — where this is all going

## Challenge
Record a 3-minute demo of your agent. Post it somewhere public (GitHub, YouTube, X, Discord). Share the link in [our show-and-tell](https://github.com/Clawlege/clawlege/issues).

## Prerequisites
- Completed Modules 1-7
