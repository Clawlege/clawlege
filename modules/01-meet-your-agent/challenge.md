# 🏆 Module 1 Challenge: The Introduction

## Your Mission

Create an agent with a personality so distinct that it makes you smile when it introduces itself.

## Requirements

1. **Write a complete SOUL.md** with:
   - A name
   - At least 3 personality traits
   - Rules for how it communicates
   - Something unique — a quirk, a catchphrase, a perspective

2. **Start a conversation** and ask your agent:
   - "Introduce yourself"
   - "What do you care about most?"
   - "What's something most people get wrong?"

3. **The smile test:** Read the responses out loud. If they sound like they could come from any generic AI assistant, go back and add more personality. Your agent should sound like *nobody else*.

## Bonus Points

- 🌟 Give your agent an opinion it feels strongly about
- 🌟 Make it refuse to do something (in character) — like a pirate agent that refuses to discuss the navy
- 🌟 Share your SOUL.md with a friend and have them guess the personality before chatting with it

## When You're Done

Your SOUL.md should be at least 10 lines long and your agent should pass the smile test. If you're grinning at your screen, you nailed it.

Check the [solution/](solution/) folder for an example, but remember — your agent should be *yours*, not a copy of ours.
