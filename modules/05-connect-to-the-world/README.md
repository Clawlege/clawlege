# Module 4: Connect to the World

**⏱ Time: ~60 minutes | 🟡 Difficulty: Intermediate | 💻 Coding: Minimal**

Your agent lives in a terminal. Time to break it out.

---

## What You'll Learn

- **Channel connections** — How agents communicate through iMessage, Discord, Telegram, and more
- **Real-time data** — Connecting to live information sources (news, stocks, social media)
- **Proactive behavior** — Teaching your agent to reach out *to you* instead of waiting to be asked
- **Heartbeats** — How agents check in periodically and do useful background work
- **Safety and permissions** — Why your agent asks before sending messages (and why that matters)

## Module Outline

### Lesson 1: Channels
- What are communication channels?
- Setting up your first channel (iMessage or Discord)
- Sending and receiving messages through your agent
- The difference between "chat mode" and "channel mode"

### Lesson 2: Proactive Agents
- The heartbeat system — periodic check-ins
- Writing a HEARTBEAT.md file
- Configuring what your agent monitors
- When to reach out vs. when to stay quiet

### Lesson 3: External Skills
- Installing community skills from ClawHub
- Google Workspace (email, calendar, drive)
- GitHub integration
- Building your agent's toolkit

### Lesson 4: Safety First
- Permission models — ask-first vs. auto-approve
- Allowlists and blocklists
- Why your agent shouldn't send emails without asking
- Building trust gradually

## Challenge Preview
Set up your agent on a real communication channel and have it send you a proactive message based on something happening in the real world (weather alert, calendar reminder, news update).

## Prerequisites
- Completed Modules 1-3
- Access to at least one messaging platform (iMessage, Discord, or Telegram)
