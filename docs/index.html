Frequently Asked Questions

What is Clawlege?

Clawlege is a free, open source interactive course that teaches anyone to build AI agents using OpenClaw. It has 9 modules covering everything from writing your first agent personality to deploying on hardware, plus 5 starter templates you can use immediately.

Is it really free?

Yes. 100% free, forever. All 9 modules and 5 starter templates are available at no cost. The entire course is open source on GitHub under MIT license. No signup required.

Do I need coding experience?

No. Modules 1–4 require zero coding — you write your agent's personality in plain English. Intermediate modules (5–7) introduce connecting to real services and hardware. Advanced modules (8–9) cover shipping and presenting your agent.

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open source AI agent framework that lets you build personal AI assistants that can remember you, search the web, manage your calendar, and run on your phone. Clawlege teaches you how to use it, starting from zero experience.

Who is this for?

Everyone. Kids and teens can build a homework helper or sports tracker. Curious adults can understand AI by building it themselves. Developers can skip to Module 5 for custom skills, hardware integrations, and production-ready agents.

How long does it take?

Module 1 takes about 30 minutes. The full course is roughly 8 hours. You can go at your own pace — each module is self-contained.

Ready to build your agent?

It takes 30 minutes and zero coding knowledge. Seriously.
